Working Groups

HISTORY

HKBCS Working Groups were established for various topics in which blockchain and trust technologies are most relevant today, catalysing deep learning and collaboration between subject-matter experts and leaders. HKBCS Working Groups organize meetups and may publish position papers on a quarterly basis.

HKBCS Talent Development Working Group

The Talent Development Working Group of the Hong Kong Blockchain Society is responsible for working with educators, academics, institutions, research institutes, and other talent development groups.

HKBCS Healthcare Technology Working Group

The Healthcare Technology Working Group of the Hong Kong Blockchain Society is responsible for working with healthcare professionals, medical personnel, pharmaceutical companies, insurance companies, policy makers, and other stakeholders for a healthy society.

HKBCS Regulation Technology Working Group

The Regulation Technology Working Group of the Hong Kong Blockchain Society is responsible for working with professionals and policy makers in regulated industries from around the world, such as but not limited to supply chain, finance, health, insurance, land and real estate, government, education, construction, etc.

About Us

The Hong Kong Blockchain Society is dedicated to raising awareness of the Blockchain Technology beyond the technologists to the public, industry, regulators and policy makers.
