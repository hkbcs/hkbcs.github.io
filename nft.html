NFT Hackathon

The NFT hackathon series helps students gain a deeper understanding in non-fungible tokens. This series is exclusively for students participating in the HSBC/CityU Fintech Olympiad and the Hong Kong Blockchain Olympiad.

Day 1

NFT Meetup

Fri 3 Dec at 7pm

Learning outcomes: upon completion, students would be able to:

  1. Meet other like-minded students interested in exploring NFTs;
  2. Meet mentors who have real-world experience with NFTs;
  3. Get to know about the history of and current trends in NFTs;
  4. Get to know about the use of NFTs beyond art and media.
Register

Day 2

NFT Briefing

Fri 10 Dec at 7pm

Learning outcomes: upon completion, students would be able to:

  1. Understand what is (non)fungibility and its relevancy;
  2. Understand what is an NFT and the history of its development;
  3. Understand what is Algorand and how to create NFT on Algorand;
  4. Understand basic systems theory and design thinking.
Register

Day 3

NFT Minting

Mon 13 Dec at 7pm

Learning outcomes: upon completion, students would be able to:

  1. Setup a development environment for Algorand via a virtual machine;
  2. Create Algorand accounts with public/private keys;
  3. Create a non-fungible token (NFT) asset on Algorand;
  4. Transfer a non-fungible token (NFT) asset on Algorand.
